About

Lawyer

I'm an attorney licensed to practice in NJ (where I'm from) and PA (where I lived and studied in Philadelphia for 12 years). Currently, I serve as judicial law clerk in the Court of Common Pleas in Pike County, PA. My legal interests include intellectual property, privacy, e-discovery and the loosely connected doctrines collectively referred to as "internet law." I'm also interested in helping freelancers protect themselves (and their clients) with advice on contracts, collections, intellectual property and more.

Creative

I also do freelance work of my own. I can write for your publication, build you a website or a blog, edit or rewrite your existing copy, integrate analytics or advertising into your site, and develop social media and digital marketing strategies. I can update and maintain the work I do for you or teach you to do it yourself, depending on your interests and needs. My rates are reasonable and I'll even refrain from condescending to you or using silly jargon. Win-win.
